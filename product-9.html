Foam Putty

Price: Rs.00

Foam Wall Putty is an innovative, lightweight wall finishing compound designed to provide a smooth and durable surface on interior walls and ceilings. Combining the benefits of traditional putty with the ease of foam application, this putty is ideal for filling cracks, holes, and minor imperfections in walls. Its foam-like consistency allows for easy application and spreading, while providing excellent adhesion and a strong, lasting finish. Foam Wall Putty is perfect for preparing surfaces before painting, ensuring a flawless final look.

Key Characteristics

  • Lightweight and Easy to Apply: The foam consistency makes the putty easy to spread, reducing application time and effort.
  • Smooth Finish: Dries to a smooth, even surface that is ideal for painting and other wall finishes.
  • Strong Adhesion: Bonds well to a variety of surfaces, including plaster, concrete, and drywall, ensuring a secure and lasting finish.
  • Crack and Shrink Resistant: Once dry, the putty resists cracking and shrinking, providing a durable, long-lasting finish.
  • Quick Drying: Dries faster than traditional putties, allowing for quicker completion of wall preparation and finishing tasks.
  • Paintable Surface: Can be easily painted over once cured, ensuring seamless integration with the rest of the wall surface.

Scope of Use

  • Wall and Ceiling Repair: Ideal for filling cracks, holes, and minor imperfections in interior walls and ceilings.
  • Pre-Painting Preparation: Prepares walls for painting by providing a smooth, even surface that enhances the final paint finish.
  • Drywall Finishing: Suitable for finishing drywall joints and seams, providing a uniform surface ready for paint or wallpaper.
  • Interior Applications: Designed for use on interior walls and ceilings, particularly in living rooms, bedrooms, and other residential spaces.
  • DIY and Professional Use: Suitable for both DIY enthusiasts and professional painters, offering an easy-to-use solution for wall finishing.

Instructions for Use

  1. Surface Preparation: Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and free from dust, grease, and loose material. Remove any flaking paint or deteriorated material from the application area.
  2. Application: Apply Foam Wall Putty using a putty knife or trowel. Spread the putty evenly over the surface, filling any cracks, holes, or gaps. Smooth the surface to achieve a uniform finish.
  3. Drying: Allow the putty to dry completely. Drying time can vary based on the thickness of the application and environmental conditions, typically within 4-6 hours.
  4. Sanding: Once dry, lightly sand the surface with fine-grit sandpaper to achieve a smooth, even finish. Make sure to remove any dust before proceeding to the next step.
  5. Painting or Finishing: After sanding, the surface is ready for painting or other finishes. Follow the paint manufacturer's instructions for best results.
