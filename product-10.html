Oil-Based Putty

Price: Rs.00

Oil-Based Wall Putty is a high-performance compound specifically designed for interior and exterior surfaces requiring a smooth, durable finish. Formulated with oil-based ingredients, this putty provides excellent adhesion and a long-lasting, glossy surface, making it ideal for preparing walls, ceilings, and woodwork before painting. Its oil-based formulation ensures enhanced durability, making it resistant to wear and tear, moisture, and environmental factors. Perfect for areas that demand a tough and resilient finish, Oil-Based Wall Putty is the professional's choice for achieving a polished, high-quality look.

Key Characteristics

  • Smooth and Glossy Finish: Delivers a highly smooth and polished surface, ideal for achieving a professional-grade finish that enhances the final paint coat.
  • High Durability: Provides a tough and resilient layer, suitable for areas prone to wear and tear, ensuring long-lasting protection.
  • Strong Adhesion: Bonds well to various surfaces like plaster, brick, stone, and wood, providing a solid foundation for paint and other finishes.
  • Moisture Resistant: Offers resistance to moisture, making it suitable for interior and exterior applications where exposure to damp conditions is a concern.
  • Ready for Paint: Once dried, the putty forms a strong, smooth base that is perfect for painting with oil-based or latex paints.
  • Versatile Application: Ideal for use on walls, ceilings, woodwork, and other surfaces requiring a durable, smooth finish.

Scope of Use

  • Interior Walls and Ceilings: Perfect for preparing surfaces before painting, providing a smooth and durable base.
  • Woodwork and Furniture: Suitable for filling gaps, cracks, and imperfections in wood surfaces, ensuring a flawless finish.
  • Exterior Surfaces: Can be used on exterior walls to provide a protective and smooth base for paint, enhancing weather resistance.
  • Pre-Painting Surface Preparation: Ideal for use before applying oil-based or latex paints, ensuring a smooth and even final coat.
  • Restoration Projects: Excellent for use in restoration work, providing a durable base for painting and finishing on aged surfaces.

Instructions for Use

  1. Surface Preparation: Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and free from loose material, grease, dirt, or any other contaminants. Sand the surface lightly if needed to improve adhesion.
  2. Application: Stir the Oil-Based Wall Putty thoroughly before use. Apply the putty using a putty knife or trowel, spreading it evenly over the surface to fill any cracks, holes, or imperfections.
  3. Drying: Allow the putty to dry completely, which can vary depending on the thickness of the application and environmental conditions, typically within 6-8 hours.
  4. Sanding: Once dry, sand the surface with fine-grit sandpaper to achieve a smooth, even finish. Wipe away any dust before proceeding to the next step.
  5. Painting: After sanding, the surface is ready for painting. Apply a primer if necessary, then proceed with your chosen paint. Follow the paint manufacturer's instructions for best results.
