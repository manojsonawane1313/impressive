Ready-Mix Putty

Price: Rs.00

Ready-Mix Putty is a pre-mixed, easy-to-use compound designed to deliver a smooth and durable finish on interior and exterior walls. Ideal for repairing cracks, holes, and surface imperfections, this putty requires no mixing and is ready to apply straight from the container. Its high adhesion and smooth texture make it perfect for preparing walls before painting or wallpapering. Suitable for use on a variety of surfaces, Ready-Mix Putty simplifies the process of achieving a flawless wall finish.

Key Characteristics

  • Convenient Pre-Mixed Formula: Ready to use straight out of the container, saving time and ensuring consistent results.
  • Smooth Finish: Provides a flawless, even surface, ideal for creating a professional-grade finish.
  • High Adhesion: Bonds strongly to various surfaces, including plaster, concrete, and drywall, enhancing the durability of the finish.
  • Easy Application: Applies smoothly with a putty knife, requiring minimal effort and sanding.
  • Quick Drying: Dries efficiently, allowing for faster completion of projects.
  • Versatile Use:Suitable for both interior and exterior walls, offering a wide range of applications.

Scope of Use

  • Crack and Hole Repair: Ideal for filling cracks, holes, and surface imperfections in walls and ceilings.
  • Surface Preparation: Prepares walls for painting or wallpapering, ensuring a smooth and even base.
  • Interior and Exterior Applications: Versatile for use on various wall surfaces in both interior and exterior environments.
  • Patchwork and Touch-Ups: Perfect for small repairs and touch-ups, making it a go-to solution for maintenance and renovation projects.

Instructions for Use

  1. Surface Preparation: Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and free from dust, grease, and loose material. Remove any flaking paint or deteriorated material before application.
  2. Application: Using a putty knife, apply the Ready-Mix Putty directly to the surface imperfections. Smooth it out to ensure an even layer that blends seamlessly with the surrounding area.
  3. Drying: Allow the putty to dry completely. The drying time may vary depending on the thickness of the application and environmental conditions, typically drying within 1-2 hours.
  4. Sanding: Once dry, lightly sand the surface with fine-grit sandpaper to achieve a smooth, even finish. Remove any sanding dust before proceeding with painting or additional coats.
  5. Painting or Finishing: After sanding, the surface is ready for painting or other finishes. Follow the paint or wallpaper manufacturer's instructions for the best results.
