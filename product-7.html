Epoxy Putty

Price: Rs.00

Epoxy Putty is a powerful, versatile repair compound designed for a wide range of applications. Known for its exceptional bonding strength and durability, Epoxy Putty can be used to repair, fill, and rebuild various materials, including metal, wood, concrete, and plastic. Once mixed, it forms a strong, permanent bond that hardens like steel, making it ideal for both structural and cosmetic repairs. Whether for home repairs, industrial maintenance, or DIY projects, Epoxy Putty provides a reliable solution that lasts.

Key Characteristics

  • High Strength Bonding: Provides an incredibly strong bond that can withstand heavy loads, impacts, and stress.
  • Versatile Application: Suitable for use on a variety of materials, including metal, wood, concrete, ceramic, and plastic.
  • Quick Setting: Hardens rapidly after mixing, allowing for quick repairs and minimal downtime.
  • Waterproof and Chemical Resistant: Once cured, the putty is resistant to water, chemicals, and extreme temperatures, making it ideal for harsh environments.
  • Moldable and Sandable: Can be molded into shape before it hardens and sanded or drilled once cured for precise repairs and customizations.
  • Long-Lasting Durability: Offers a permanent repair solution that maintains its strength and integrity over time.

Scope of Use

  • Structural Repairs: Ideal for repairing and reinforcing metal structures, pipes, and machinery.
  • Filling Cracks and Holes: Perfect for filling large cracks, holes, and voids in wood, concrete, and metal surfaces.
  • Rebuilding and Reshaping: Can be used to rebuild broken or missing parts, such as metal components, ceramic fixtures, and wood details.
  • Waterproof Sealing: Effective for sealing leaks and cracks in plumbing, tanks, and outdoor structures exposed to water.
  • Industrial and Home Applications: Suitable for use in both industrial settings and home repairs, offering a versatile solution for various needs.

Instructions for Use

  1. Surface Preparation: Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and free from rust, grease, dirt, and loose material. Roughen smooth surfaces to enhance adhesion.
  2. Mixing: Cut or break off the required amount of epoxy putty. Knead the putty by hand until the color is uniform, indicating that the components are fully mixed.
  3. Application: Apply the mixed putty to the repair area within the working time (typically 3-5 minutes). Press firmly to ensure good contact with the surface. Shape or mold as needed before the putty starts to harden.
  4. Curing: Allow the putty to cure fully. Depending on the product, full curing may take 1-2 hours, after which the repair will be ready for sanding, drilling, or painting.
  5. Finishing: Once cured, the epoxy putty can be sanded, drilled, painted, or machined as needed to achieve the desired finish.
