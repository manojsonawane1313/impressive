Polyester Putty

Price: Rs.00

Polyester Wall Putty is a high-performance filler designed for preparing and smoothing walls and ceilings before painting. Made from advanced polyester resin, this putty offers exceptional adhesion and durability. It effectively fills cracks, holes, and surface imperfections, providing a seamless base for painting. The quick-drying formula allows for rapid application and finishing, making it an excellent choice for both professional and DIY projects.

Key Characteristics

  • Durable Finish: Provides a long-lasting, strong surface that enhances the durability of paint applications.
  • Fast Drying: Dries quickly, allowing for faster project completion and reducing wait times between coats.
  • High Adhesion: Offers excellent bonding to various substrates, ensuring a solid foundation for paint.
  • Smooth Application: Easy to apply with a putty knife or applicator, resulting in a smooth, even surface with minimal sanding required.
  • Crack Resistance: Maintains flexibility and resists cracking, ensuring a stable finish even with minor surface movements.
  • Water Resistant: Suitable for both interior and exterior use with resistance to moisture and weather conditions.

Scope of Use

  • Filling Cracks and Holes: Ideal for repairing small cracks, holes, and imperfections in walls and ceilings.
  • Surface Preparation: Prepares various wall surfaces, including plaster, concrete, and drywall, for painting.
  • Interior and Exterior Applications: Effective for use in both interior and exterior environments, providing a versatile solution for different surfaces.
  • Smoothing Textured Surfaces: Can be used to even out textured or uneven surfaces before applying paint.

Instructions for Use

  1. Surface Preparation: Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and free of dust, grease, and loose debris. Remove any flaking paint or deteriorated material.
  2. Application: Apply the polyester wall putty using a putty knife or applicator, filling cracks, holes, and imperfections. Smooth the putty evenly to ensure it blends seamlessly with the surrounding surface.
  3. Drying: Allow the putty to dry thoroughly. Drying time may vary depending on the thickness of the application and environmental conditions, but it generally dries within 1-2 hours.
  4. Sanding: Once dry, sand the surface lightly with fine-grit sandpaper to achieve a smooth, even finish. Remove any sanding dust before proceeding with painting.
