Elastic Putty

Price: Rs.00

Elastic Putty is a high-performance, flexible compound designed to fill and repair cracks, joints, and gaps in various surfaces. Its unique elastic properties allow it to accommodate movement without cracking, making it ideal for surfaces that experience frequent expansion and contraction. Elastic Putty is perfect for both interior and exterior applications, providing a durable and long-lasting solution for a wide range of repair and finishing tasks.

Key Characteristics

  • Flexible Finish: Maintains elasticity after drying, preventing cracks and ensuring long-lasting performance even on moving surfaces.
  • Strong Adhesion: Bonds effectively to a variety of substrates, including wood, plaster, concrete, and metal.
  • Weather Resistant: Suitable for exterior use, offering resistance to moisture, UV rays, and temperature fluctuations.
  • Easy Application: Applies smoothly with a putty knife or applicator, providing a seamless finish with minimal effort.
  • Crack Resistant: Designed to resist cracking, even in areas subject to frequent movement or temperature changes.
  • Paintable Surface: Can be painted over once fully cured, allowing for seamless integration with the surrounding surface.

Scope of Use

  • Crack and Joint Repair: Ideal for filling and sealing cracks, joints, and gaps in walls, ceilings, and other surfaces.
  • Exterior and Interior Use: Versatile for use in both interior and exterior environments, including areas exposed to the elements.
  • Flexible Surfaces: Perfect for surfaces that experience movement, such as doors, windows, and exterior walls.
  • Sealing Applications: Effective for sealing joints in wooden structures, concrete surfaces, and metal elements.

Instructions for Use

  1. Surface Preparation: Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and free of dust, grease, and loose material. Remove any flaking paint or deteriorated material before application.
  2. Application: Apply the Elastic Putty using a putty knife or applicator. Fill the crack, joint, or gap completely, and smooth the surface to blend with the surrounding area.
  3. Drying and Curing: Allow the putty to dry and cure fully. Depending on the thickness of the application and environmental conditions, this may take 24-48 hours.
  4. Sanding (Optional) If necessary, lightly sand the surface with fine-grit sandpaper to achieve a smooth finish. Ensure the surface is dust-free before painting or finishing.
  5. Painting or Finishing: Once fully cured, the Elastic Putty can be painted or finished according to your preferences. Follow the paint or finish manufacturer's instructions for best results.
