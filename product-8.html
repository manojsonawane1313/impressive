Waterproof Putty

Price: Rs.00

Waterproof Putty is a specialized, water-resistant compound designed to provide a durable and smooth finish on walls, ceilings, and other surfaces exposed to moisture. Perfect for both interior and exterior applications, this putty offers superior adhesion and long-lasting protection against water penetration. It is ideal for areas prone to dampness, such as bathrooms, kitchens, and exterior walls. Easy to apply and sand, Waterproof Putty ensures a flawless finish that stands up to the elements.

Key Characteristics

  • Waterproof Finish: Forms a water-resistant barrier that protects surfaces from moisture damage and prevents the growth of mold and mildew.
  • Superior Adhesion: Bonds effectively to a variety of surfaces, including concrete, plaster, brick, and wood, ensuring a strong and durable finish.
  • Smooth Application: Spreads easily with a putty knife or trowel, allowing for a seamless finish with minimal effort.
  • High Durability: Resistant to cracking and peeling, even in high-moisture environments, ensuring long-lasting protection.
  • UV and Weather Resistant: Suitable for exterior use, offering resistance to UV rays, rain, and other weather conditions.
  • Ready for Paint: Can be painted over once cured, allowing for a smooth and attractive final finish.

Scope of Use

  • Damp Areas: Ideal for use in bathrooms, kitchens, and other areas where moisture resistance is essential.
  • Exterior Walls: Perfect for exterior wall applications, providing a protective layer against rain and humidity.
  • Waterproofing Repairs: Suitable for repairing and filling cracks, holes, and gaps in surfaces exposed to water.
  • Pre-Painting Surface Preparation: Prepares surfaces for painting by providing a smooth, water-resistant base.

Instructions for Use

  1. Surface Preparation: Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and free of dust, grease, and loose material. Remove any existing paint or deteriorated material in the application area.
  2. Application: Apply the Waterproof Putty using a putty knife or trowel. Spread the putty evenly over the surface, filling any cracks, holes, or gaps. Smooth the surface to achieve a uniform finish.
  3. Drying: Allow the putty to dry completely. Drying time can vary based on the thickness of the application and environmental conditions, typically within 6-8 hours.
  4. Sanding: Once dry, lightly sand the surface with fine-grit sandpaper to achieve a smooth, even finish. Make sure to remove any dust before proceeding to the next step.
  5. Painting or Finishing: After sanding, the surface is ready for painting or other finishes. Follow the paint manufacturer's instructions to ensure optimal results.
