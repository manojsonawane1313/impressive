Key Characteristics

  • Smooth Finish: Ensures a flawless and even surface, perfect for achieving a high-quality paint finish.
  • Quick Drying: Dries rapidly, allowing for faster application and minimal downtime between coats.
  • Excellent Adhesion: Provides a strong bond to various surfaces, enhancing paint adhesion and durability.
  • Easy Application: Smooth and easy to apply with a putty knife or applicator, requiring minimal sanding.

  • Water Resistant: Offers resistance to moisture, making it suitable for both interior and exterior applications.

Scope of Use

  • Crack and Hole Repair: Ideal for filling small cracks, holes, and surface imperfections in walls and ceilings.
  • Surface Preparation: Prepares surfaces for painting, ensuring a smooth and even base for a professional finish.
  • Interior and Exterior Walls: Suitable for use on plaster, concrete, drywall, and other wall surfaces in both interior and exterior environments.
  • Textured Surfaces: Can be used to smooth out textured or uneven surfaces before painting.

Instructions for Use

  1. Surface Preparation: Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and free from dust, grease, and loose material. Remove any loose or flaking paint before application.
  2. Application: Using a putty knife or applicator, apply the acrylic wall putty to the cracks, holes, or imperfections. Smooth the putty evenly and ensure it is well-filled and level with the surrounding surface.
  3. Drying: Allow the putty to dry completely. The drying time may vary depending on the thickness of the application and environmental conditions. Typically, it dries within 1-2 hours.
  4. Sanding: Once dry, lightly sand the surface with fine-grit sandpaper to achieve a smooth, even finish. Remove any sanding dust before painting.
