White Cement-Based Putty

Price: Rs.00

White cement wall putty is a fine white powder made from white cement and polymers, designed for use on plastered walls. It creates a smooth, uniform surface, ready for painting. This product is known for its excellent adhesion, moisture resistance, and smooth finish, providing a durable base for the application of paints.

Key Characteristics

  • High Adhesion: Adheres strongly to the substrate, preventing the paint from flaking off.
  • Smooth Finish: Provides a smooth surface, ideal for high-quality paint applications.
  • Moisture Resistance: Resists moisture, preventing damage like peeling or dampness.
  • Durability: Extends the life of paint by providing a stable base.
  • Alkaline Resistance: Prevents the paint from being affected by the alkalinity of the cement.
  • Eco-Friendly: Non-toxic and environment-friendly.

Scope of Use

  • Interior and Exterior Walls: Suitable for both indoor and outdoor surfaces.
  • Pre-Paint Application: Applied before painting to ensure a smooth, even surface.
  • Concrete and Plastered Walls: Ideal for use on plastered walls, concrete, and even ceilings.
  • Home and Commercial Spaces: Used in residential buildings, offices, and other commercial establishments.

Instructions for Use

  1. Surface Preparation: Ensure the wall surface is clean, dry, and free from dust, grease, or loose particles.
  2. Mixing: Mix thoroughly to obtain a smooth, lump-free paste. Let it stand for 10 minutes, then remix.
  3. Application: Apply the first coat of wall putty using a putty knife or trowel, with an even, uniform thickness.
  4. Finishing: After the putty has completely dried, the surface can be sanded again to achieve an ultra-smooth finish.
  5. Storage: Store the product in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and moisture.
